# Instructions for Invoice & Receipt Processor

This guide provides instructions on how to use the Invoice & Receipt Processor application.

## What It Does

This tool is a digital assistant that automates the tedious process of managing invoices and receipts. You upload your financial documents, and the application will automatically:

*   **Read the document** using Optical Character Recognition (OCR).
*   **Extract key information** like the vendor, date, and total amount.
*   **Intelligently categorize** the expense (e.g., "Office Supplies," "Travel").
*   **Rename and organize** the file into a clean, searchable folder structure.
*   **Provide a dashboard** where you can view, search, and generate reports on all your expenses.

## How to Use It

The application is a web-based dashboard, accessible at **[https://invoice.curak.xyz](https://invoice.curak.xyz)**.

### 1. Uploading a Document

1.  Navigate to the **Upload** tab.
2.  Drag and drop your invoice or receipt file (PDF, PNG, JPG) onto the upload area, or click to browse your computer.
3.  The application will begin processing the document automatically.

### 3. Reviewing and Editing

This is the most important step. The application will show you the data it extracted.

*   **Verify the information:** Check that the Vendor, Date, and Amount are correct.
*   **Make edits if needed:** You can click on any field to correct it.
*   **Confirm the category:** The system will suggest a category. You can change it using the dropdown menu.
*   **Add notes:** You can add any extra details in the notes field.
*   Click **"Process & File Document"** to complete the process.

### 4. Managing Your Expenses

Navigate to the **Expenses** tab to see all your processed documents. Here you can:
*   **Search:** Use the search bar to find specific expenses.
*   **Filter:** Filter your view by category, vendor, or a specific date range.

### 5. Generating Reports

Navigate to the **Reports** tab to get insights into your spending. You can:
*   Generate reports that group your expenses by category or vendor.
*   See monthly spending trends.
*   Export your reports to a **CSV file** for use in spreadsheets or other accounting software.

This tool helps you stay organized by transforming your messy pile of receipts into a structured, searchable, and insightful digital archive.
